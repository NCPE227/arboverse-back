ARBODATA

ARBOVERSE is a data-drive platform that integrates information on arboviruses, vectors, and environmental data from multiple sources and allows easy search, correlation analysis, and visualization. The ARBOVERSE was designed to help accelerate the work of researchers, public health workers, and the global community to better prepare for, respond to, and reduce the burden of arboviruses

This data set combined models with spatio-temporal projections of explanatory variables (at 0.25 degrees spatial resolution) from the year 1970 to 2050 under five Shared Socioeconomic Pathways (SSPs) to project the Biodiversity Intactness Index (BII). Mean BII (weighted by cell area) was calculated at the country, subregion, interregion and global level. The study used cross-validation (leaving one biome out in turn) to produce decadal upper and lower uncertainty margins for 1970-2050.
